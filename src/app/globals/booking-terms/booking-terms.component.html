Getting charged for damage
Accidents are rare, but they happen. If you, someone you invite, or a pet are responsible for damage during a stay, your payment method may be charged.
What can I be charged for?
You could be charged for damage, any of your Proprietor’s stuff that goes missing, or unexpected cleaning costs due to your stay.
What’s the process?
If you and your Proprietor can’t work it out first, we’ll step in to determine responsibility. We’ll only charge your payment method if we have reason to believe you’re responsible.
What if I don’t agree?
You’ll have a chance to appeal if you think we made a mistake.
